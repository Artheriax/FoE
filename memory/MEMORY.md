## 2. The Power Ceiling Hierarchy
Power is strictly capped by race. The only ways to break this ceiling are:
1. Being born with a Gaze.
2. Being born with a Sigil.
3. Consuming/Harvesting Essence from Monsters.

**Racial Hierarchy (Lowest to Highest):**
Humans < Mortal Races < Middle Races < High Races < Celestials -> Primordials -> Ascendants -> Eternarcs -> Higher Ones

## 3. Institutional Authority
- **MOD (Magic Oversight Division):** The bureaucratic public authority. Registers hunters (~344k active). Maintains order.
- **Eternal Guard:** The cosmic/secret authority. Handles extinction-level threats, Nihility, Entity Zero. Supersedes the MOD.
- **Guilds:** The operational arms (e.g., Aethelgard Consortium, Solaris Vanguard, Umbra Shroud).

## 4. Unresolved Mysteries & Reserved Variables
- **Entity Zero:** The pre-intentional substrate of all sources. All research is classified/sealed.
- **Wall of Nihility:** An inverted boundary. Keepers of Nil are closely tied to it. Artem walks out of this in Book V.
