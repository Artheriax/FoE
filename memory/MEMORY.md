# AI Core Memory / Rulebook & State Tracker

*Core statistics, overarching rules, and population calibration for the Fragments of Eternity universe. This file ensures AI agents and co-pilots maintain consistent facts about the world of Hyperion.*

---

## World Overview

- **Primary World:** Hyperion — a fusion of arcane energy and advanced technology
- **Power Ceiling:** Capped by race; most beings cannot exceed their racial ceiling
- **Cosmology:** Layered universe (Layer 0 = material universe, Layer +N = dimensions outside the universe, Layer -N = strata deeper within the universe)
- **Key Institution:** Magic Oversight Division (MOD) — Hyperion's largest corporation; regulates hunters, cross-layer trade, descent licensing, artifact importation, and tournament sanctioning

## Power Systems

- **Three Sources:**
  - **Vitalis** — Physical (body)
  - **Psyche** — Mental (mind)
  - **Aeterium** — Cosmic (Higher Ones only)
- **Arcane Schools:** Elemental, Holy/Unholy, Keystone, Echo/Enchantment, Forbidden
- **Layered Lord System:** Each layer (positive or negative) may have lords who embody concepts/elements. Succession by combat (gift or kill-and-take-place) or by lord-on-lord violence (airborne magic selects a new bearer). Each lord has messengers and champions.

## Racial Hierarchy

- **Higher Ones / Upper Lords** — Cosmic forces of creation, destruction, judgment
- **Entity Zero** — Origin deeper than the laws of reality itself
- See `world/Races.md` for full hierarchy

## Key Organizations

- **MOD (Magic Oversight Division)** — Hyperion's largest corporation; cross-layer trade regulation
- **Eternal Guard** — Cosmic oversight, handles extinction-level threats
- **Keepers of Nil** — Immortal observers tied to the Wall of Nihility

## Layer Structure

- **Layer 0:** Material universe (Hyperion + other worlds)
- **Layer +N:** Dimensions outside the universe (Luminara, Gehenna, Dreamveil, The Void, etc.). Not all have lords.
- **Layer -N:** Strata deeper within the universe. Deeper = darker, more powerful, more valuable, fewer sane visitors. The Undergates = canonical entrances to Layer -1.
- See `systems/Cosmology.md` for full system rules and `world/Layers/Layer-Minus-1.md` for the first negative layer.
