# Elemental Magic

The manipulation of the world's fundamental forces. Power is drawn from internal reserves or by drawing energy from the environment. Six schools cover the primary natural forces, each with distinct military, industrial, and civilian applications.

## Elemental Schools

### Blaze Magic (Pyromancy)

Control over fire and heat. Masters can create plasma, induce spontaneous combustion, or absorb flames.

**Institutional Application:** Industrial heating, metallurgical forging, tactical fire support, Hexline Tower construction.

---

### Frost Magic (Cryomancy)

Control over ice and cold. Masters can freeze the air in lungs, create absolute zero fields, or forge unmelting glacial constructs.

**Institutional Application:** Essence containment (frozen storage facilities), evidence preservation, extreme climate research. The absolute zero field technique pioneered by Glaciarch Frostweaver underpins all modern MOD high-tier essence storage.

---

### Shock Magic (Fulminancy)

Control over lightning and electromagnetism. Masters can manipulate technology, disrupt neural signals, or teleport short distances via lightning arc.

**Institutional Application:** Hexline Tower operation and maintenance, power generation, technological interference suppression. Fulminancy specialists are among the most institutionally critical practitioners — the Hexline network requires constant supervision by trained Fulminancy practitioners.

---

### Terra Magic (Geomancy)

Control over earth, stone, and metal. Masters can cause earthquakes, magnetize armor, or create complex underground structures.

**Institutional Application:** Underground infrastructure, mining operations, structural reinforcement. Several major city subterranean districts were constructed by individual Terra masters whose identities were only established after their deaths.

---

### Radiant Magic (Photomancy)

Control over light. Distinct from Holy magic — Photomancy manipulates light's physical properties rather than its metaphysical significance. Masters can create hard-light constructs, bend light for perfect invisibility, or fire concentrated beams.

**Institutional Application:** Surveillance technology, hard-light barrier construction, classified weaponization research. Note: Photomancy is sometimes confused with Luminancy by non-practitioners; they are distinct schools with no theological relationship.

---

### Umbral Magic (Umbrakinesis)

Control over darkness and shadows. Distinct from Unholy magic — Umbrakinesis manipulates the absence of light rather than Void-derived dark energy. Masters can travel through shadows, create zones of sensory deprivation, or solidify darkness into weapons.

**Institutional Application:** Elite hunter support, stealth operations, shadow-phase reconnaissance. Enhanced oversight requirements: practitioners monitored for potential Deep Umbraturgy overlap, though the two schools are mechanically distinct.

## Forces

Forces are not spells but the fundamental expressions of a Higher One's will. They are not learned; they are wielded. A mortal attempting to channel even a fraction of this power would be instantly unmade.

- **The One:** The confluence of all Higher Ones, embodying absolute cosmic authority and the totality of divine power.
- **The Outer One:** Force of the Beyond, the spaces outside the known universe, the creation of the Aether and all things alien and incomprehensible.
- **The Destroying One:** Force of Entropy, Annihilation, Unmaking.
- **The Creating One:** Force of Genesis, Potential, Manifestation.
- **The Judging One:** Force of Absolute Truth, Law, Consequence.
- **The Balancing One:** Force of Equilibrium, Cause and Effect, Cosmic Counterweight.
- **The Intervening One:** Force of Aberration, the Unforeseen Variable, Divine Intervention.
- **The Observing One (Phariah):** Force of Record, Unchanged Memory, Silent Witness.
- **The Nonexistent One:** Force of Erasure, the Un-Concept, the Void Before and After.
- **The Nameless One:** Force of Anonymity, the Forgotten Detail, Identity Theft on a cosmic scale.
- **The Forgotten One:** Force of Oblivion, erased from all memory and record, existing only as an absence.
