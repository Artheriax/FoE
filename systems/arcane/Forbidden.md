# Forbidden Magic

Forms of magic so dangerous or antithetical to reality that their use is banned by the MOD and hunted by specialized enforcement. Six schools are designated forbidden, ranging from theoretical prohibition to extinction-level classification.

Any practitioner confirmed to use forbidden magic is subject to immediate apprehension and termination of citizenship rights. Several schools carry death-on-sight orders.

## Nihility Magic

**Discovered:** Pre-Record era, exact date unknown. First documented evidence recovered from archaeological site at the Fracturing of the Eastern Rift.

**Summary:** Only three confirmed successful casts in recorded history, all Pre-Record era. The casters are identified in classified archives by location and outcome only; their names were consumed by the casting. The first — designated *Caster Alpha* — produced a localized erasure of a settlement whose name is no longer recoverable from any existing record; the settlement's physical location still exists but carries no structural trace of habitation. The second — *Caster Beta* — erased themselves and a Keystone-class monster simultaneously; the monster's classification and threat level are unknown because the records of it were consumed alongside the caster. The third — *Caster Gamma* — is theorized by some scholars to be associated with the Cataclysm of Yggdra, though the records required to confirm or refute this were consumed in the casting. Secondary effects from all three casts created localized reality distortions lasting decades in the affected regions; the Eastern Rift fracture zone carries residual Nihility traces that dimensional survey teams use as calibration reference points.

**Institutional Response:** Classified as highest-tier forbidden. Any evidence of attempted study results in immediate execution and memory purge of all institutional knowledge of the practitioner. Current containment protocol: Seal all theoretical texts in classified vaults. No MOD personnel may research or compile data on this school.

**Cross-Reference:** The Cataclysm of Yggdra (theory: Nihility involvement; classification sealed)

The attempt to wield the "nothingness" of the Nihility dimension. It doesn't destroy; it erases. A single successful cast typically consumes the caster entirely, wiping them from history. It is the magic of absolute self-sacrifice and oblivion.

## Wall of Nihility "Magic"

**Discovered:** Ongoing since Pre-Record era. No successful interaction documented.

**Summary:** The Wall exhibits no response to any known magical manipulation. Three major research initiatives — the *Velthorne Expedition* (Third Era), the *Meridian Academy Study Group* (Fifth Era), and the *Seventh Era Theoretical Survey* under Dravenmore's coordination — all terminated within 18 months due to personnel casualties. The Velthorne Expedition lost all seven researchers to progressive cognitive disruption; five were recovered but never regained functional awareness. The Meridian Academy Study Group produced the most detailed pre-ban research corpus before four of its six members developed symptoms consistent with permanent dimensional displacement. The Seventh Era Survey's findings were sealed before publication; one researcher from that group, *Acolyte Thessen*, is the only individual in recorded history to have approached the Wall's active engagement threshold and returned with coherent documentation — a 12-page field journal that is sealed in classified archives and has never been made available to MOD's Theoretical Research Division. Thessen retired from all research activity within three months of the expedition's conclusion.

**Institutional Response:** Absolute research ban. "Wall interaction study" classified as capital offense. Any material older than containment protocols is destroyed. Current knowledge held only by the highest classified authorities.

It is believed no magic can affect the Wall. It is not a thing to be manipulated, but a law of reality. Research into it is forbidden because it invariably leads to madness or attracts the attention of things that should not be noticed.

## Nil Space Magic

**Discovered:** Theoretical only. Proposed by Archon Dravenmore (Spatial Magic master) in Seventh Era correspondence.

**Summary:** Hypothetically, Nil Space represents the conceptual "containment" of nothingness — if Nihility is emptiness, Nil Space is the imprisonment of that emptiness. No confirmed attempt to access it; all theoretical pathways produce mathematical paradoxes.

**Institutional Response:** Dravenmore case classified and terminated via permanent institutional reassignment. All correspondence and research notes sealed. Cross-discipline theoretical discussion forbidden. Any practitioner proposing Nil Space mechanics is immediately flagged for psychological evaluation and investigation.

**Cross-Reference:** `systems/arcane/Keystone.md` — Archon Dravenmore (sealed entry); Classified research tag applied.

A theoretical magic. If Nihility is nothingness, Nil Space is the prison for nothingness. Accessing it is considered impossible. Myths say a being who could wield its power would be able to imprison concepts like "hope" or "time" itself.

## Shadow Magic (Deep Umbraturgy)

**Discovered:** Third Era, documented case of the Veiled Operations.

**Summary:** Three documented practitioners across recorded history attempted communion with Void entities. **Case 1** (Third Era, *Practitioner Astrid Veyne*): possession, entity inhabitation resulted in reality distortion event localized to Ashenveil district, producing approximately four hundred casualties across a three-month active period before containment. Veyne's body was recovered; the entity was not. **Case 2** (Mid-Era, *Subject Meridian*): partial mutation during uncontrolled Maltheryx-adjacent dimensional exposure. Meridian was retrieved and contained; the mutation to their Psyche structure was not reversible. Active research ongoing under seal. **Case 3** (Modern Era, identity sealed): practitioner attempted sustained Void entity communion using Shadow Magic as the contact medium. Outcome classified at the highest authority. The sealed file notation reads: *"Contact achieved. Entity designation unresolved. Practitioner status pending."*

**Institutional Response:** Deep Umbraturgy practitioners are subject to immediate quarantine and execution if confirmed possessed. All known practitioners in institutional records are monitored with scrying protocols. Void entity contact automatically escalates to the highest classified authority.

**Cross-Reference:** The Veiled Operations (Third Era).

Going beyond controlling darkness into communing with and drawing power from the entities that dwell in the deepest Void. This always comes with a price: possession, mutation, or becoming an anchor for a Maltheryx or worse.

## Soul Forging

**Discovered:** Fourth Era, practitioner: Mage Callax of Nethermore. Case terminated by execution and entire settlement quarantine.

**Summary:** Callax developed technique for soul fragmentation and essence extraction. Documented 47 victims before apprehension. Souls fractured beyond recovery; victims left in cataleptic state. Practitioner developed extreme power surge before execution — 9 enforcers required to contain final casting.

**Institutional Response:** Soul Forging classified as capital crime worse than homicide. Any instrumental evidence (soul fragments, forged essence bottles) is immolated without study. Practitioners are executed on sight and their remains are cremated with purification rituals. Theoretical study forbidden; all texts destroyed.

**Cross-Reference:** Callax the Rendered (Fourth Era, sealed entry).

The art of tearing a soul apart and using its raw energy to power spells or enchantments. It is the most reviled form of magic, punishable by eternal imprisonment or execution.

## Reality Fabrication

**Discovered:** Sixth Era, practitioners: Archmongers Thessalyn and Korven. Joint experimental timeline rewrite attempt.

**Summary:** Thessalyn and Korven attempted to alter a single historical event involving Hexline Tower construction failure. Instead created 47 intersecting parallel timelines, each progressively unstable. Collapse cascade resulted in existential space rupture affecting three continental zones. Reality damage persists: approximately 4,000 km² remain temporally fractured. Return expeditions result in severe temporal psychosis in survivors.

**Institutional Response:** Reality Fabrication classified as extinction-level forbidden magic. All practitioners immediately executed. Related research destroyed across all institutional records. Cross-timeline study sealed in classified archives. Current containment: reality breach quarantined; no expeditions authorized.

**Cross-Reference:** The Sixth Era Collapse (sealed); Temporal Fracture Zones (restricted access).

Attempting to do what only the Creating One or similarly cosmic beings can do. History records several catastrophes where powerful mages tried to rewrite a single event and instead created unstable parallel timelines that collapsed, causing widespread existential damage.
