# Keystone Magic

Keystone Magic is not a school but a classification for spells that manipulate reality's most complex and fundamental aspects. Accessing this magic requires a Keystone Essence and is incredibly dangerous. Most who attempt it without a natural affinity (like a Celestial or Higher One) risk their mind and soul.

High-tier practitioners require MOD licensing and periodic evaluation. Keystone school users represent the most heavily regulated category within the Arcane Registry.

- **Chronomancer Timerion** (Second Era) — Temporal Magic pioneer; developed the theoretical framework for Moment's Recall and demonstrated feasibility of Hasten/Slow at scale. Disappeared during unauthorized Temporal Stasis experiment. Status: Presumed lost to temporal fracture.

## Temporal Magic

Manipulation of time. Even minor spells are incredibly draining.

**Institutional Application:** Highly restricted; used only in Eternal Guard consultation for historical validation. No standing MOD licenses issued for offensive Temporal applications.

- **Hasten/Slow:** Affects the flow of time for a single target or small area.
- **Moment's Recall:** Allows the caster to rewind time by a few seconds for themselves only, effectively giving them a second chance at a single action. Extremely disorienting.
- **Temporal Stasis:** Places a target in a bubble of stopped time. The energy cost scales exponentially with the size of the target and duration.

## Spatial Magic

Manipulation of space and distance.

**Institutional Application:** Hexline system (Archon Dravenmore active oversight), interdimensional containment, pocket dimension management. The Hexline infrastructure represents the largest single application of Spatial Magic in Hyperion's history — a lifetime project by a single Archmage-level practitioner.

- **Teleportation:** Personal teleportation is complex but possible for masters. Teleporting others or objects is far more dangerous.
- **Spatial Fold:** Creates a pocket dimension. Most are small and temporary, used for storage. Creating a stable, habitable pocket dimension is a feat for a Primordial.
- **Dimensional Gate:** The principle behind Hexlines. Creating a stable gate requires immense, fixed infrastructure (Hexline Towers).

## Cosmic Magic

Manipulation of celestial forces, gravity, and fate.

**Institutional Application:** Strategic Starfall support for elite hunters, gravitic manipulation for containment structures. Fateweave is a forbidden branch of this school.

- **Gravity Well:** Increases or decreases gravity in a localized area.
- **Starfall:** Pulls a fragment of astral energy from the heavens to strike a target.
- **Fateweave:** A forbidden branch that attempts to see or alter the threads of destiny. The backlash is always severe and unpredictable. All documented attempts resulted in severe psychological fracturing or death. Research forbidden; listed practitioners registered and monitored.
