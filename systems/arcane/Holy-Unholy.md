# Holy and Unholy Magic

The two primary metaphysical schools — Luminancy drawing from Luminara and the essence of creation, Umbraturgy drawing from Gehenna and the Void. They are anathema to each other in application, though both remain fully registered and regulated within MOD's Arcane Registry.

*→ `systems/Arcane.md` — Index: MOD Registry Overview | All Schools | Forbidden Magic*

---

## Holy Magic (Luminancy)

The magic of Luminara, fueled by faith, purity, and the essence of creation. It is anathema to corruption and decay.

### Lesser Luminancy

- **Luminous Orb:** Creates a ball of harmless, bright light.
- **Soothing Touch:** Minor healing of cuts and bruises.
- **Ward of Purity:** Creates a small barrier that weakens minor unholy effects.

### Greater Luminancy

- **Sanctify Ground:** Consecrates an area, making it difficult for unholy creatures to enter or remain.
- **Radiant Lance:** A spear of solidified light that sears flesh and disorients dark beings.
- **Greater Restoration:** Heals poisons, diseases, and crippling wounds.

### Celestial Luminancy

- **Resurrection:** An immensely complex and draining ritual to return a recently deceased soul to its body. Requires the soul's willingness and an intact body.
- **Judgment's Gaze:** The caster's eyes glow with divine light, forcing a target to confess their greatest sins or be momentarily paralyzed by guilt.
- **Heaven's Fall:** Calls down a devastating pillar of pure holy energy to obliterate a target.

## Unholy Magic (Umbraturgy)

The magic of Gehenna and the Void, fueled by ambition, pain, and the essence of negation. It is often faster and more destructive but can corrupt the user.

### Notable Practitioners

- **Magister Valdross the Profane** (Pre-Third Era) — Mastered Profane Umbraturgy; attempted to create a permanent plague-zone in the Nardec district using Mass Enthrall thralls as vectors. Operation halted by Eternal Guard intervention at cost of 4,000+ lives. Validation Date: Executed by celestial fire, year of Judging. Case remains classified but referenced in forbidden magic restriction ordinances.
- **Corvus Nightwhisper** (Fifth Era) — Anonymous Soul Shackle specialist under MOD contract for paranormal containment. Amplitude: Greater. Active 47 years, sealed institutional record.
- **The Void-Touched Collective** (Modern Era) — Underground network of Greater Umbraturgy practitioners. MOD active surveillance, 3 known cells eliminated since Seventh Era. Location: Sealed.

### Lesser Umbraturgy

- **Shadow Tendril:** Creates a whip of darkness to grasp or strike.
- **Minor Hex:** Causes bad luck, minor paranoia, or a sense of dread in a target.
- **Siphon Life:** Drains a small amount of vitality from a target to heal the caster's minor wounds.

### Greater Umbraturgy

- **Soul Shackle:** Temporarily binds a soul, preventing resurrection or communication with the afterlife.
- **Hellfire Eruption:** Unleashes a burst of green or black fire that burns both physically and spiritually.
- **Abyssal Possession:** Allows the caster's consciousness to inhabit and control a weak-willed or injured creature.

### Profane Umbraturgy

- **Plague of Gehenna:** Summons a virulent, magical disease that spreads rapidly and is resistant to conventional healing.
- **Mass Enthrall:** Subverts the will of multiple weaker beings, turning them into loyal thralls.
- **Annihilating Ray:** A thin, black beam that does not burn or freeze, but simply unmakes matter it touches, leaving behind absolute nothingness.
