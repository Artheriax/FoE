# Absolute Gazes

**MOD Classification:** Confirmed mortal Absolute Gaze bearers exist — Eternal Guard working estimates place the active population at approximately 7,500 across all Absolute Gaze types — but every such case is classified above MOD access level at Eternal Guard authority and does not appear in standard Registry records. MOD's publicly accessible position remains: no confirmed mortal Absolute bearers in general Registry files. Containment of any confirmed case would exceed standard MOD capacity; all engagement authority is reserved to the Eternal Guard. Cross-reference is permitted between MOD Theoretical Research Division and Eternal Guard archives for verified research purposes only.

**Academic Status:** Absolute Gazes remain the subject of intense theological and academic debate. Some deny their existence entirely, classifying historical accounts as mythology. Others treat their potential existence as fundamental to understanding cosmic balance.

**Regulatory Framework:** Any confirmed discovery of an Absolute Gaze bearer triggers automatic escalation to Eternal Guard authority. MOD authority is explicitly limited to advisory capacity in such scenarios.

## Absolute Gaze of Oblivion

Wielded by the Destroying One. This Gaze does not destroy matter; it erases the concept of the target from existence. The target is removed from the past, present, and future. No one remembers they ever existed, and all evidence of their life vanishes.

True eyes of void. They are not black, but the absence of everything — a hole in reality where eyes should be.

**Case Origin:** The oldest confirmed Oblivion case in Eternal Guard records dates to the Pre-Record era, attributed to a figure known only as *Karthyn the Undoing.* Unlike most Pre-Record figures whose deeds are reconstructed from physical evidence, Karthyn's existence is attested by a negative trace: an Elven Tree of Life record that references a community elder by name and position, with the space immediately after the name describing the elder's contributions entirely blank — not erased, not redacted, but structurally absent from the record as though the concept of those contributions had been removed from the document's logic. Cross-referencing older records reveals the elder did exist, did make documented contributions, and those contributions vanished from all records simultaneously with the Tower of Valdas event. The Crimson Tower at Valdas, a significant Pre-Record structure, was reportedly unmade by the bearer rather than allowed to fall into enemy hands. Physical evidence confirms the tower ceased to exist rather than collapsed. Karthyn's fate is unrecorded — consistent with Oblivion's documented tendency to consume its bearer at the final activation.

**Modern Bearer Status:** Eternal Guard records list three active confirmed Oblivion cases, all sealed above MOD access. One is reportedly held in voluntary containment at an Eternal Guard facility; the other two are classified as uncontained and monitored.

**Status:** Confirmed historical and active.

## Absolute Gaze of Infinity

Wielded by the Creating One or The One. This Gaze perceives and can manipulate the threads of time, space, and causality on a universal scale. It can rewind time, create alternate realities, or unmake laws of physics.

The eyes contain an ever-expanding, perfect model of the entire cosmos within them, with galaxies being born and dying in the space of a blink.

**Case Origin:** The Fourth Era produced the only documented Infinity activation in Eternal Guard archives: a figure identified in surviving records as *Elowen the Unwoven.* Elowen was a human temporal theorist whose research into Keystone-class Time magic led to a breakthrough that elevated a Revelation Gaze through Absolute Truth into an unstable Infinity state — the first and only documented case of a three-step Gaze evolution. The activation lasted approximately nine seconds of external time, during which Elowen perceived all of Hyperion's past and future timelines simultaneously, attempted to document what she saw, and was disabled by a Judging One intervention before the perception could be weaponized. Nine of her notebooks survived. Their contents are sealed in Eternal Guard archives; field notes from the investigating team describe them as "consistent with someone attempting to describe a four-dimensional object using a two-dimensional vocabulary." Elowen survived the activation but lost coherent speech for the remaining two years of her life. Her notebooks contain a single legible line on the final page: *"The end of the pattern is the beginning of the pattern. The same face. He was always going to walk out."* MOD's Theoretical Research Division has cross-referenced this with Artem's emergence and reached no formal conclusion.

**Modern Bearer Status:** No active Infinity cases are confirmed in Eternal Guard records. The Judging One's intervention in Elowen's case is interpreted by some scholars as establishing a precedent: Infinity may not be wielded by mortals. Whether this is prohibition or practical limitation is unresolved.

**Status:** Confirmed historical (one documented case).

## Absolute Gaze of Absolute Truth

Wielded by the Judging One. This Gaze forces universal truth upon any who meet it. Liars are forced to speak only truth, illusions shatter, and beings are confronted with the unchangeable facts of their existence and actions.

The eyes are made of pure, blinding white light. There are no pupils or irises, only two points of absolute, judgmental luminance.

**Case Origin:** The primary documented historical bearer is a Second Era figure known only as *"the Arbiter of Ithra."* A MOD predecessor body convened a tribunal to adjudicate a territorial dispute between three noble houses involving contested Gaze-bearer inheritance claims — a case that had resisted resolution for eleven years. The Arbiter was called as a witness. During testimony, the Arbiter met each claimant's gaze in sequence and stated aloud the contents of their intentions: one house had fabricated evidence, another had employed a devil to suborn witnesses, and the third had been pursuing a legitimate claim through compromised channels. All three claimants confirmed the statements before the tribunal — not because they chose to, but because the Gaze of Absolute Truth was active and their bodies could not produce falsehood in its presence. The tribunal issued its ruling within the hour. The Arbiter left the proceedings immediately afterward and was never located by any institution for follow-up. The ruling remains the foundation for MOD's current Gaze-bearer inheritance law, codified as the Ithra Precedent.

**Modern Bearer Status:** Eternal Guard records list fewer than ten active Absolute Truth bearers. One is known to serve as a standing advisor to the Eternal Guard's judgment proceedings; the others are listed as unaffiliated and monitored. MOD has no jurisdiction over any of them.

**Status:** Confirmed historical and active.

## Absolute Gaze of Soul Sovereignty

Wielded by higher cosmic entities. This Gaze sees the soul itself and can command it. The user can enslave, liberate, reshape, or devour souls with a thought, commanding all life energies and consciousness.

The eyes are shifting, ethereal, and translucent, like looking through a window into the Astral Plane. Within them, countless faint soul-lights flicker and move.

**Case Origin:** The earliest surviving reference is a Pre-Cataclysm text fragment held in the Elven Tree of Life sealed archive, which describes an entity known as *"the Keeper of Names"* — a figure who could perceive a soul's true designation and, by speaking it, compel the soul's owner to any single action. The fragment is incomplete; the outcome of the encounter is not preserved. The Compact Defiant Adjudication (Second Era) indirectly references Soul Sovereignty as a theoretical counterforce to the Soul Anchoring Sigil — but the Compact Defiant's own ruling established that Soul Anchoring overrides extraction mechanisms regardless of the extracting force's tier, and this has never been tested against a confirmed Sovereignty bearer. Eternal Guard records classify the question as "theoretically unresolved, practically untested." The one recorded claim of active Soul Sovereignty possession during the recorded era — a figure known as *Rava the Claimant,* who allegedly seized control of twelve individuals simultaneously during the Mid-Era Ashenmoor Campaigns — was investigated and determined inconclusive by MOD auditors. Rava disappeared before a secondary evaluation could be attempted.

**Status:** Two confirmed historical cases, no confirmed active.

## Absolute Gaze of Reality Fabrication

The pinnacle of the Illusion Gaze. This Gaze does not hijack perception; it rewrites local reality. If the user gazes at a barren field and imagines a forest, a forest becomes real. The effect is permanent unless dispelled by an equal power.

The eyes are a constantly changing mosaic of every color and pattern imaginable, flickering with impossible geometries and unrealized possibilities.

**Case Origin:** A confirmed Reality Fabrication activation occurred during the Sixth Era, documented in Eternal Guard case files as the Sixth Era Collapse. Practitioners *Thessalyn* and *Korven*, joint bearers of the evolved Illusion line, attempted to rewrite a single historical event — a Hexline Tower construction failure that had caused a regional transit collapse. The attempt produced 47 intersecting parallel timelines rather than a single rewrite, each progressively unstable, resulting in a collapse cascade that fractured approximately 4,000 km² of territorial fabric. The Eternal Guard quarantined the affected region permanently. Thessalyn and Korven's current status is classified at Eternal Guard authority; MOD records list them as "unresolved."

**Modern Bearer Status:** Eternal Guard records list an undisclosed number of active cases, all sealed. The Sixth Era quarantine zone remains under Eternal Guard jurisdiction. No fabric re-stabilization has been achieved in the affected region.

**Status:** Confirmed historical and active.

## Absolute Gaze of Mindscape

This Gaze allows the user to project their consciousness into the minds of entire populations simultaneously, creating a shared dream or nightmare that they control absolutely. They can communicate with a world or drive it mad in an instant.

The pupils are vast, deep, and reflective, like still water at night. Anyone who looks into them sees their own reflection, but it moves independently of them.

**Case Origin:** The Sleeper Incident, initiated in the Seventh Era and ongoing through the Modern Era. A Mindscape bearer — identity sealed at Eternal Guard authority — embedded themselves within MOD's Gaze and Sigil Assessment Unit and operated undetected for an extended period, projecting controlled dream states into MOD personnel and civilians across multiple population centers. The extent of affected individuals is not publicly documented. The incident was addressed by the Echo Breakers in Book VII, culminating in a transmission to the Eternal Guard that identified the bearer's institutional position and active methodology. The bearer was subsequently neutralized in Book VIII by Artem under Eternal Guard authorization. The dreamwork inflicted on affected individuals — including Echo Breaker Lyra — did not reverse upon the bearer's neutralization; Artem's application of a mimicked and reversed Mindscape Gaze was required for extraction.

**Modern Bearer Status:** The Sleeper Incident bearer is neutralized. Eternal Guard records indicate fewer than five additional active Mindscape cases, all sealed. The incident prompted a full restructuring of MOD's Assessment Unit internal security protocols — the single largest institutional reform triggered by a Gaze event in the Modern Era.

**Status:** Confirmed historical and active.

## Absolute Gaze of Cosmic Judgment

The final form of the Judging One's power. This Gaze can instantly assess a being's entire life, their essence, and their impact on the cosmic balance, and deliver an immediate, unappealable sentence of annihilation or salvation.

One eye is the blinding white of Absolute Truth, the other is the void of Oblivion. Together, they represent the binary choice of judgment.

**Case Origin:** The only documented instance of Cosmic Judgment activation in Eternal Guard records occurred during the Callax Abomination case (Fourth Era). When Mage Callax was executed for Soul Forging by Eternal Guard intervention, the record notes what scholars have subsequently identified as a Cosmic Judgment manifestation: Callax's sentence was reportedly pronounced by a figure whose eyes matched the described appearance of Cosmic Judgment — one blinding white, one void — and whose verdict was neither deliberated nor appealed but simply delivered, as a statement of fact rather than a decision. The figure was not an Eternal Guard member. The figure did not remain for documentation. The Eternal Guard's own report on the Callax execution includes a single notation, in the lead investigator's handwriting, reading: *"External adjudicator present. Verdict issued. No further action required."* Whether this represents a Higher One's direct intervention, an independent Cosmic Judgment bearer operating at Higher One directive, or a Guard member whose eyes manifested the composite effect temporarily is unresolved. The Callax incident remains the only case where Cosmic Judgment is referenced in a document not classified as theoretical.

**Status:** One confirmed historical activation; no confirmed active bearer.
