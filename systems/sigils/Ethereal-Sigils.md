# Ethereal Sigils

Ethereal Sigils are not enhancements or redefinitions. They are statements about the nature of the body's relationship with existence. A bearer at this tier does not have a stronger or more protected body. They have a body that the normal rules of things are no longer fully authorized to apply to.

Only one confirmed living Ethereal Sigil bearer exists in any accessible record. The others either predate documentation, are classified beyond accessible registry tiers, or exist in records that have themselves been classified.

## Ethereal Sigil of Reality Immunity

**Effect:** The bearer's body and soul are recognized as a fixed constant across all versions of the multiverse. They are completely immune to reality-altering effects, temporal rewrites, paradoxes, and dimensional erasure. When timelines are altered or erased, the bearer remembers. When reality is rewritten around them, they persist unchanged. They exist as a point that all versions of the multiverse agree cannot be removed.

**Appearance:** The Sigil is not located on the skin but manifests as a faint, perfectly still second silhouette — a "true self" outline that overlaps the bearer's body and never moves regardless of what the bearer does. When the bearer moves, the silhouette does not initially follow; a fraction of a second later, it relocates as though confirming a position rather than tracking motion.

**Lore:** The theoretical framework for Reality Immunity requires postulating that bearers exist simultaneously in all versions of the multiverse as a fixed reference point. Timeline erasure cannot affect them because there is no version of reality in which they did not exist: they are one of the premises, not one of the conclusions. Paradoxes don't contradict them; they resolve around them, the universe adjusting its internal logic to accommodate the bearing fact of the bearer's existence.

This also means that beings with reality-altering powers — certain Keystone monsters, Absolute Gaze wielders, entities with temporal authority — encounter a specific limitation when interacting with Reality Immunity bearers. They can rewrite the world around the bearer. They cannot rewrite the bearer themselves. The bearer will simply remain, unchanged, in whatever reality has been constructed around them, and will remember what preceded it.

The only figure in Hyperion's historical record who is credibly linked to Reality Immunity is referenced in three separate ancient texts from three separate cultures spanning what would become Hyperion's modern scope, referring to the same figure as, respectively, *"the one who remained"*, *"She Who Did Not Change"*, and *"the Fixed Point."* The accounts span events separated by approximately four hundred years, with a consistent physical description in each, and record this figure as present at major reality-destabilization events: the Cataclysm of Yggdra, the Fracturing of the Eastern Rift, and the collapse of what the oldest account calls "the Second Weave." In each case the accounts are consistent. Everything around the figure was altered, rewritten, or erased. The figure was not. Did not appear to particularly notice. The account of the Eastern Rift specifically notes that the figure expressed mild inconvenience at the path forward being obscured. All three accounts describe the figure continuing on their way once the event concluded.

No record exists of where they went after. Whether this is because they are no longer present in the current era, or because they simply have not yet appeared in an event significant enough to produce written record, is unknown. The MOD's classified theoretical office has a single open working file on the subject. Its status is: *ongoing.*

## Ethereal Sigil of Eternal Physique

**Effect:** The body becomes a permanent vessel, capable of weathering any physical environment, injury, or stress indefinitely without degradation. It could drift through a supernova or the absolute-zero conditions of Nihility and remain structurally intact and biologically functional. Physical destruction, at any scale short of fundamental cosmic unmaking, simply does not produce lasting damage.

**Appearance:** The bearer's flesh takes on the quality of divine materials — seeming, to the naked eye, like polished living stone, ancient celestial bronze, or unbreakable old-growth wood in certain lights. Glowing Sigil-patterns are etched permanently into this new surface. Unlike lower-tier Sigils whose appearance shifts with activity, the Eternal Physique marks are constant: always visible, always present, as though the skin itself is the Sigil.

**Lore:** Ancient records contain a recurring figure described as a being "with skin like carved starlight" who walked through the Cataclysm of Yggdra — one of the most devastating events in Hyperion's chronicled history — and emerged unmarked from the other side. Scholars contest whether this describes an Eternal Physique bearer or a Primordial in temporary mortal form. The description's detail and the figure's subsequent recorded actions suggest something that was not simply observing from safety but was actively present in the catastrophe and simply did not register it as harmful.

The practical reality of bearing Eternal Physique has no fully documented equivalent to draw on. The only inference available is that the body stops being something that threats are measured against and starts being something that threats are measured relative to.

## Ethereal Sigil of Dimensional Anchoring

**Effect:** The bearer is anchored absolutely to their home dimension and timeline. They cannot be banished, teleported against their will, pulled into pocket dimensions, or displaced into alternate realities through any mechanism. Their presence also stabilizes the physical fabric of dimensional space around them, preventing localized rifts and distortions from forming in their immediate vicinity.

**Appearance:** Complex anchor-like symbols glow at the bearer's feet and palms, with faint chains of light connecting them to whatever surface they stand on. The chains are visible to magical perception and faintly visible even to the naked eye in dim conditions, appearing as luminous threads that don't quite fully vanish when the bearer moves.

**Lore:** Devils encounter a specific and infuriating problem with proven Dimensional Anchoring bearers. Standard infernal contract enforcement includes exile and banishment clauses — mechanisms for removing an uncooperative bearer from any given location. Those clauses have no effect on a Dimensional Anchoring bearer: the instruction to remove them from their dimension is issued, and then literally nothing happens, because the bearer's position is not subject to that kind of revision. Several infernal houses have standing policies of identifying and avoiding suspected Dimensional Anchoring bearers entirely rather than committing resources to contract enforcement that cannot function.

For dimensional exploration teams, these bearers serve as living anchors — their presence guaranteeing that whatever drift or distortion a hostile dimension introduces, the immediate space around the bearer remains stable. Being near one in an unstable dimensional environment is described as standing on the only solid ground in an earthquake.

## Ethereal Sigil of Cosmic Harmony

**Effect:** Aligns the bearer's body with the fundamental constants of the universe. They become immune to entropy, decay, and energy dissipation on a physical level. They age only deliberately, if they choose to. They draw all necessary sustenance directly from ambient cosmic background radiation. The body becomes, in effect, a self-sustaining system that requires nothing from its environment to continue functioning and loses nothing to it.

**Appearance:** The bearer's Sigils shift into a dynamic display: a perfect miniature representation of a solar system or galaxy, with the bearer's body positioned at the center. The orbiting patterns glow with the soft, constant light of a distant star, and shift subtly over time as the cosmic display evolves.

**Lore:** Bearers of Cosmic Harmony stop experiencing biological need in any conventional sense. Hunger does not occur. Thirst does not occur. The sustenance provided by cosmic background radiation is described by the only theoretical accounts available as a continuous "fullness without source" — not absence of appetite but absence of the framework in which appetite arises. The body is simply sufficient, always, without input.

This makes them functionally independent of any planetary support system, which creates both tactical and philosophical complications. They cannot be starved, dehydrated, isolated from resources, or worn down by deprivation. They also have no particular relationship with the social rituals that surround eating, resting, and biological care — the parts of daily life that form the texture of connection between people. The gaps created by this are different from those of Endurance bearers, who still remember needing things. Cosmic Harmony bearers, over time, may stop emotionally registering those needs as real because they have become biologically unreal.

## Ethereal Sigil of Transcendent Adaptation

**Effect:** The bearer's physiology evolves automatically and permanently to counter any threat that makes contact with it or credibly threatens its existence. The adaptation is not temporary and does not require the original threat to remain present to maintain. If cut by a blade, the skin becomes harder than the blade. If struck by fire, the body becomes permanently immune to heat. The scope of potential adaptation is, in theory, unlimited — encompassing all other Sigil feats as possible outcomes of sufficient adaptive pressure.

**Appearance:** The Sigil has no fixed form. It is a blank, silvery-white surface on the bearer's skin that morphs in real time, instantly forming and dissolving counter-Sigil patterns in direct response to whatever force is currently acting on the bearer. When no active threat exists, the surface is smooth and featureless. When threatened, it becomes briefly, brilliantly active — and then settles into its new configuration and goes still again.

**Lore:** The Adaptation Sigil at Standard and Supreme tiers follows predictable rules. It responds to environments. It calibrates to conditions. It can be understood and, to some extent, mapped. At the Ethereal tier, those rules stop applying in any consistent way.

The only confirmed bearer, Artem, developed the Transcendent Adaptation through research — the application of his original gaze-and-sigil formula to himself through Nihility-adjacent magic and modern biological mutations that did not exist when the original formula was created. His body has incorporated properties from this self-applied fusion, producing adaptations the current magical-scientific framework cannot fully categorize. Several of its current configurations appear to have emerged during the development process rather than through environmental threat response — the Sigil adapting to its own creation conditions rather than external pressure. He does not have a complete inventory of what his own body is capable of, which he finds professionally interesting and occasionally inconvenient.

The Adaptation lineage is unique among Sigil progressions in one specific respect: there is no documented Absolute Sigil of Adaptation. Every other lineage has a clear Standard → Supreme → Absolute → Ethereal chain. Adaptation has a gap. The scholarly theory — held primarily by researchers who have connected enough classified sources to see the lineage clearly — is that Adaptation cannot be *capped*. An Absolute Sigil requires a defined ceiling: a specific pinnacle of immunity, strength, or protection. Adaptation refuses that ceiling by definition. A Sigil that adapts without limit cannot be crystallized into a fixed Absolute form. It either remains within the Standard-to-Supreme range, or it breaks directly through to the Ethereal, bypassing the Absolute tier entirely. **Artem's** existence is the **only** data point for the Ethereal variant. For now, he is both the proof and the case study.
