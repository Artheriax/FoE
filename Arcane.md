Arcane

*The Arcane Systems of Hyperion*

The Arcane Systems of Hyperion

Magic on Hyperion is the art of shaping the ambient energy that
permeates all existence. This energy is neutral, but the method of
shaping it and the intent behind it define its school and effect. The
most common form of magic is learned and cast through intricate
gestures, spoken words (arcane formulae), and mental focus. However,
some beings, particularly higher races or those with unique bloodlines,
can wield magic through sheer force of will.

Spells (Arcane Formulae)

Spells are the structured, repeatable application of magical energy.
They are categorized by their nature and effect.

Holy Magic (Luminancy)

The magic of Luminara, fueled by faith, purity, and the essence of
creation. It is anathema to corruption and decay.

Lesser Luminancy

-   Luminous Orb: Creates a ball of harmless, bright light.

-   Soothing Touch: Minor healing of cuts and bruises.

-   Ward of Purity: Creates a small barrier that weakens minor unholy
    effects.

Greater Luminancy

-   Sanctify Ground: Consecrates an area, making it difficult for unholy
    creatures to enter or remain.

-   Radiant Lance: A spear of solidified light that sears flesh and
    disorients dark beings.

-   Greater Restoration: Heals poisons, diseases, and crippling wounds.

Celestial Luminancy

-   Resurrection: A immensely complex and draining ritual to return a
    recently deceased soul to its body. Requires the soul\'s willingness
    and an intact body.

-   Judgment\'s Gaze: The caster\'s eyes glow with divine light, forcing
    a target to confess their greatest sins or be momentarily paralyzed
    by guilt.

-   Heaven\'s Fall: Calls down a devastating pillar of pure holy energy
    to obliterate a target.

Unholy Magic (Umbraturgy)

The magic of Gehenna and the Void, fueled by ambition, pain, and the
essence of negation. It is often faster and more destructive but can
corrupt the user.

Lesser Umbraturgy

-   Shadow Tendril: Creates a whip of darkness to grasp or strike.

-   Minor Hex: Causes bad luck, minor paranoia, or a sense of dread in a
    target.

-   Siphon Life: Drains a small amount of vitality from a target to heal
    the caster\'s minor wounds.

Greater Umbraturgy

-   Soul Shackle: Temporarily binds a soul, preventing resurrection or
    communication with the afterlife.

-   Hellfire Eruption: Unleashes a burst of green or black fire that
    burns both physically and spiritually.

-   Abyssal Possession: Allows the caster\'s consciousness to inhabit
    and control a weak-willed or injured creature.

Profane Umbraturgy

-   Plague of Gehenna: Summons a virulent, magical disease that spreads
    rapidly and is resistant to conventional healing.

-   Mass Enthrall: Subverts the will of multiple weaker beings, turning
    them into loyal thralls.

-   Annihilating Ray: A thin, black beam that does not burn or freeze,
    but simply unmakes matter it touches, leaving behind absolute
    nothingness.

Elemental Magic

The manipulation of the world\'s fundamental forces. Affinity is often
innate but can be learned with great difficulty. Power is drawn from
internal reserves or by drawing energy from the environment.

-   Blaze Magic (Pyromancy): Control over fire and heat. Masters can
    create plasma, induce spontaneous combustion, or absorb flames.

-   Frost Magic (Cryomancy): Control over ice and cold. Masters can
    freeze the air in lungs, create absolute zero fields, or forge
    unmelting glacial constructs.

-   Shock Magic (Fulminancy): Control over lightning and
    electromagnetism. Masters can manipulate technology, disrupt neural
    signals, or teleport short distances via lightning arc.

-   Terra Magic (Geomancy): Control over earth, stone, and metal.
    Masters can cause earthquakes, magnetize armor, or create complex
    underground structures.

-   Radiant Magic (Photomancy): Control over light. Distinct from Holy
    magic, it manipulates light\'s physical properties. Masters can
    create hard-light constructs, bend light for perfect invisibility,
    or fire lasers.

-   Umbral Magic (Umbrakinesis): Control over darkness and shadows.
    Distinct from Unholy magic, it manipulates the absence of light.
    Masters can travel through shadows, create zones of sensory
    deprivation, or solidify darkness into weapons.

Keystone Magic

This is not a school but a classification for spells that manipulate
reality\'s most complex and fundamental aspects. Accessing this magic
requires a Keystone Essence and is incredibly dangerous. Most who
attempt it without a natural affinity (like a Celestial or Higher One)
risk their mind and soul.

Temporal Magic

Manipulation of time. Even minor spells are incredibly draining.

-   Hasten/Slow: Affects the flow of time for a single target or small
    area.

-   Moment\'s Recall: Allows the caster to rewind time by a few seconds
    for themselves only, effectively giving them a second chance at a
    single action. Extremely disorienting.

-   Temporal Stasis: Places a target in a bubble of stopped time. The
    energy cost scales exponentially with the size of the target and
    duration.

Spatial Magic

Manipulation of space and distance.

-   Teleportation: Personal teleportation is complex but possible for
    masters. Teleporting others or objects is far more dangerous.

-   Spatial Fold: Creates a pocket dimension. Most are small and
    temporary, used for storage. Creating a stable, habitable pocket
    dimension is a feat for a Primordial.

-   Dimensional Gate: The principle behind Hexlines. Creating a stable
    gate requires immense, fixed infrastructure (Hexline Towers).

Cosmic Magic

Manipulation of celestial forces, gravity, and fate.

-   Gravity Well: Increases or decreases gravity in a localized area.

-   Starfall: Pulls a fragment of astral energy from the heavens to
    strike a target.

-   Fateweave: A forbidden branch that attempts to see or alter the
    threads of destiny. The backlash is always severe and unpredictable.

Forces

Forces are not \"spells\" but the fundamental expressions of a Higher
One\'s will. They are not learned; they are wielded. A mortal attempting
to channel even a fraction of this power would be instantly unmade.

-   The Destroying One: Force of Entropy, Annihilation, Unmaking.

-   The Creating One: Force of Genesis, Potential, Manifestation.

-   The Judging One: Force of Absolute Truth, Law, Consequence.

-   The Balancing One: Force of Equilibrium, Cause and Effect, Cosmic
    Counterweight.

-   The Intervening One: Force of Aberration, the Unforeseen Variable,
    Divine Intervention.

-   The Observing One: Force of Record, Unchanged Memory, Silent
    Witness.

-   The Nonexistent One: Force of Erasure, the Un-Concept, the Void
    Before and After.

-   The Nameless One: Force of Anonymity, the Forgotten Detail, Identity
    Theft on a cosmic scale.

Forbidden/Unusable Magic

These are forms of magic so dangerous or antithetical to reality that
their use is banned by the MOD and hunted by the Eternal Guards.

-   Nihility Magic: The attempt to wield the \"nothingness\" of the
    Nihility dimension. It doesn\'t destroy; it erases. A single
    successful cast typically consumes the caster entirely, wiping them
    from history. It is the magic of absolute self-sacrifice and
    oblivion.

-   Wall of Nihility \"Magic\": It is believed no magic can affect the
    Wall. It is not a thing to be manipulated, but a law of reality.
    Research into it is forbidden because it invariably leads to madness
    or attracts the attention of things that should not be noticed.

-   Nil Space Magic: A theoretical magic. If Nihility is nothingness,
    Nil Space is the prison for nothingness. Accessing it is considered
    impossible. Myths say a being who could wield its power would be
    able to imprison concepts like \"hope\" or \"time\" itself.

-   Shadow Magic (Deep Umbraturgy): Going beyond controlling darkness
    into communing with and drawing power from the entities that dwell
    in the deepest Void. This always comes with a price: possession,
    mutation, or becoming an anchor for a Maltheryx or worse.

-   Soul Forging: The art of tearing a soul apart and using its raw
    energy to power spells or enchantments. It is the most reviled form
    of magic, punishable by eternal imprisonment or execution by the
    Eternal Guards.

-   Reality Fabrication: Attempting to do what only the Creating One or
    an Absolute Gaze can do. History records several catastrophes where
    powerful mages tried to rewrite a single event and instead created
    unstable parallel timelines that collapsed, causing widespread
    existential damage.

Enchantments

The art of permanently imbuing objects with magical properties. It
requires rare materials, a powerful energy source (like Core Essences),
and immense skill.

Weapon Enchantments

-   Elemental Edge: Grants the weapon a permanent elemental affinity
    (Flaming, Frostbite, Shocking, etc.).

-   Soul-Drinker: The weapon stores a fraction of the life force of
    those it kills, granting the wielder enhanced vitality or healing.

-   Phase-Reaver: Allows the weapon to phase through armor or magical
    barriers to strike the target directly. Requires a Keystone Essence
    of Space.

-   Bane of \[Creature Type\]: The weapon is supernaturally effective
    against a specific type of monster or race (e.g., Bane of Umbral
    Monsters, Bane of Demons).

-   Judgment: An enchantment that can only be placed by a high-ranking
    servant of the Judging One. The weapon deals no physical damage but
    inflicts psychic pain proportional to the target\'s guilt.

Armor Enchantments

-   Aegis of Reflection: Has a chance to reflect a spell back at its
    caster.

-   Environmental Adaptation: Automatically adjusts to extreme
    temperatures and pressures.

-   Null-Field: Generates a weak anti-magic field, making the wearer
    resistant to direct magical effects.

-   Kinetic Shunt: Absorbs and disperses the force of physical impacts.

General Enchantments

-   Spatial Pocket: Creates a small, extradimensional space within an
    object (like a bag or ring) for storage.

-   Hexline Key: Allows the wielder to activate personal Hexlines for
    rapid transit across short distances (within a city).

-   Truth of the Observing One: The enchanted object (often a book or
    crystal) cannot be altered by any known means; it perfectly records
    what is spoken to it.

-   Gaze-Bound: An extremely rare and powerful enchantment that binds a
    lesser version of a Gaze\'s power to an object, like a crown that
    grants a weak form of the Gaze of Seduction.

Other Magic

Rarer types of magic or rarely used magic.

Echo Magic

The ability to generate sound and music out of nothing can be both
beautiful and painful, dominating too. It is said the music generated
with this brings a message into the souls of every being who listens to
it, but it can also be used wrongly to create darkness or sadness inside
someone\'s soul.
