# Introduction to Sigils

If Gazes are the manifestation of spiritual potential, then Sigils are
the embodiment of physical transcendence. A Sigil is a permanent,
mark-like enhancement that appears on the skin of a bearer, resembling
an intricate tattoo, a birthmark of impossible geometry, or a faint,
glowing rune. Unlike Gazes, which are purely mental and spiritual,
Sigils alter the very physics of the body they grace.

A Sigil is a biological and mystical augmentation that rewrites the
bearer\'s physical limitations. It is also an innate birth trait, even
rarer than Gazes. Where a Gaze user must learn to focus their power, a
Sigil bearer\'s power is always on, a passive constant that requires no
conscious effort to maintain.

The prevailing theory among Hyperion\'s scientific community is that
Sigils represent a perfect synergy between mortal form and the ambient
energy of the world. They are like pre-installed, biological
\"circuits\" that optimize the body far beyond its natural evolutionary
limits. A warrior born with the Sigil of Fortitude doesn\'t just train
to be strong; their muscles, tendons, and bones are fundamentally
structured to be stronger, to endure more, and to recover faster than
any normal biology should allow.

## Categories of Sigils

- **Standard Sigils:** These enhance the body's core attributes: strength, speed, senses, endurance, and adaptability. They are the foundation of physical superiority.

- **Supreme Sigils:** These represent a mastery over the body's functions, allowing for rapid regeneration, preternatural reflexes, and the ability to permanently adapt to even the most hostile environments.

- **Absolute Sigils:** These Sigils begin to defy biology entirely, granting immunity to external forces, halting aging, and even anchoring the soul itself against metaphysical attack. They are considered mythical.

- **Ethereal Sigils:** The theoretical pinnacle of physical existence. These Sigils would not just enhance the body but redefine its relationship with reality itself, granting immunity to cosmic laws, true immortality, and an evolutionary adaptability that responds to any threat instantly.

To be born with a Sigil is to be destined for physical greatness. In the
MOD, a Sigil bearer\'s rank within their racial tier is almost always
high, as their physical capabilities are considered \"cheat codes,\"
giving them a permanent advantage in combat and survival. Like Gazes,
they are highly sought after by guilds and feared by enemies, for facing
a Sigil bearer in combat is often akin to facing a natural disaster made
flesh.

# Standard Sigils

## Sigil of Fortitude

This Sigil enhances the body\'s foundational strength, granting
incredible stamina and muscle endurance. It allows for feats of
prolonged exertion that would cripple others and speeds up muscle
recovery.

A pattern of interlocking, shield-like shapes or robust geometric
patterns, often found on the shoulders, chest, or back. Glows a steady,
earthy brown or deep green when active.

## Sigil of Agility

This Sigil rewires the nervous system for peak performance, granting
lightning-fast reflexes, perfect balance, and explosive movement speed.
Bearers move with an unnatural, fluid grace.

Sleek, flowing lines and arrowhead patterns that seem to suggest motion.
Commonly appears on the legs, ankles, or around the eyes. Glows with a
swift, electric blue or yellow.

## Sigil of Senses

This Sigil dramatically heightens all five senses to superhuman levels.
The user can hear a heartbeat across a room, see with perfect clarity
for miles, and track a scent days old.

A complex, spiral or mandala pattern, often centered on the forehead or
the palms of the hands. It glows with a faint silver light, resembling a
starry night.

## Sigil of Vitality

This Sigil supercharges the body\'s natural healing processes and
fortifies the immune system. Cuts close in seconds, broken bones mend in
minutes, and the body becomes highly resistant to toxins and diseases.

A branching, tree-like or vein-like pattern that spreads out over the
body, often from the heart. It pulses with a warm, life-giving red or
pink energy.

## Sigil of Adaptation

This Sigil allows the body to temporarily alter its physiology to
survive extreme environments, such as adjusting to intense pressure,
generating heat in freezing cold, or filtering toxins from the air.

A shifting, camouflage-like pattern that seems to change slightly based
on its surroundings. It often appears on the torso or neck and glows
with a muted, adaptive grey.

## Sigil of Resilience

This Sigil reinforces the body\'s structural integrity, strengthening
bones to be nearly unbreakable and making tissues highly resistant to
cuts and impacts. It also drastically reduces the feeling of fatigue.

A pattern resembling cracked stone or unbreakable diamond latticework.
It is typically found on the knees, elbows, and fists, and glows with a
dull, sturdy grey or orange.

# Supreme Sigils

## Supreme Sigil of Regeneration

Allows for the rapid regeneration of severed limbs and vital organs. So
long as the brain or soul remains intact, the body can rebuild itself
from near-total destruction.

The vein-like patterns of the Vitality Sigil become more pronounced and
complex, glowing with an intense, vibrant crimson that pulses like a
heartbeat.

## Supreme Sigil of Endurance

The user can operate at peak capacity indefinitely, ignoring pain,
hunger, thirst, and sleep deprivation. They are the ultimate
marathoners, able to outlast any foe.

The Sigil\'s pattern becomes deeply etched and permanent, looking less
like a glow and more like tattooed light infused directly into the skin.
The color is a deep, unwavering bronze.

## Supreme Sigil of Reflex Mastery

Reaction speed becomes so enhanced that the user perceives the world in
a state of near-frozen time, allowing them to react to bullets,
supersonic attacks, and magical projectiles with ease.

The flowing lines of the Agility Sigil become incredibly dense and
intricate, covering the limbs in a network of lightning-fast patterns
that flash with bright blue light.

## Supreme Sigil of Sensory Perception

Perception expands beyond the normal five senses, allowing the user to
\"see\" in 360 degrees, perceive magical energy currents, and
intuitively sense hidden threats or weaknesses in their environment.

The spiral pattern on the forehead opens into a visible \"third eye\" of
light when active, and the palm patterns glow brightly, scanning the
environment like radar dishes.

## Supreme Sigil of Environmental Adaptation

The body\'s adaptations become permanent. The user no longer needs to
breathe, can survive in the vacuum of space, endure crushing depths, or
walk on lava without any conscious effort.

The Sigil\'s pattern fades to a barely visible scar, only flaring to
life with a powerful, deep blue glow when an environment is so extreme
it still requires active adaptation.

# Absolute Sigils

## Absolute Sigil of Immunity

Grants complete immunity to non-physical threats: poisons, diseases,
curses, psychic attacks, and harmful radiation. The body exists in a
state of perfect purity.

The Sigil manifests as a perfect, shimmering halo of light around the
part of the body it protects, or as a full-body aura of faint white
light that rejects impurities.

## Absolute Sigil of Vital Continuity

Halts aging and grants immunity to degenerative effects. The user\'s
body remains at its biological peak forever, untouched by time or decay.

The user\'s skin becomes flawless and seems to hold an inner light. The
Sigil itself is invisible, its effect seen only in the user\'s ageless
appearance.

## Absolute Sigil of Perfect Balance

Maintains perfect biological and mental homeostasis under any condition.
The user requires no oxygen, can think clearly under immense
gravitational pressure or psychic assault, and their body will never
fail due to external stress.

A perfectly symmetrical, intricate geometric pattern that glows with a
calm, steady white light at the user\'s core (solar plexus).

## Absolute Sigil of Superhuman Reflexes

Reactions become so fast they are effectively instantaneous from any
outside perspective. The user can react to things before they even
happen by perceiving the intent behind them.

The user\'s entire body is covered in a barely perceptible, shimmering
field of distorted air, a visual effect of their nervous system
operating at light speed.

## Absolute Sigil of Soul Anchoring

Protects the user\'s soul and consciousness from all external
manipulation. They cannot be possessed, their soul cannot be ripped out,
and their mind cannot be read or controlled by any means.

A stark, chains-of-duty Sigil that glows with a profound, dark silver
light over the heart and head. It feels heavy and immovable to those who
can perceive it.

# Ethereal Sigils

## Ethereal Sigil of Reality Immunity

The user\'s body and soul are recognized as a constant in the
multiverse. They are completely immune to reality-altering effects,
temporal changes, or being rewritten by paradox. They remember erased
timelines and exist unchanged in warped realities.

The Sigil is not on the skin but is a faint, constant, and unmoving
silhouette of the user that overlaps their body---a \"true\" self that
remains constant.

## Ethereal Sigil of Eternal Physique

The body becomes an eternal vessel, capable of weathering any
environment, injury, or stress indefinitely. It could drift through a
supernova or the absolute zero of Nihility and remain intact and
functional.

The user\'s flesh takes on the quality of immortal materials---seeming
like polished divine marble, celestial bronze, or ancient, unbreakable
oak. Glowing Sigils are etched into this new \"skin.\"

## Ethereal Sigil of Dimensional Anchoring

The user is anchored to their home dimension and timeline. They cannot
be banished, teleported against their will, or pulled into alternate
realities. Their very presence stabilizes the fabric of space around
them.

Complex, anchor-like symbols glow at the user\'s feet and on their
palms, with faint, chains of light connecting them to the ground
beneath.

## Ethereal Sigil of Cosmic Harmony

Aligns the user\'s body with the fundamental constants of the universe.
They become nearly invulnerable to entropy, decay, and energy
dissipation. They age only if they choose to and can draw sustenance
directly from cosmic background radiation.

The user\'s Sigils shift to display a perfect, miniature orrery of the
solar system or galaxy, with their body at the center, glowing with the
soft light of a star.

## Ethereal Sigil of Transcendent Adaptation

The ultimate evolution of adaptation. The user\'s physiology now evolves
automatically and instantly to counter any threat that has made contact
or threatens its existence. If cut by a blade, their skin becomes harder
than the blade. If hit by a fire spell, they become immune to heat. The
adaptation is permanent. In theory it has the potential to get all other
sigils feats and abilities.

The Sigil has no fixed form. It is a blank, silvery slate on the user\'s
skin that morphs in real-time, instantly forming a new, perfect
counter-Sigil to whatever force is being applied to the user.
