# Aethelgard Consortium

*Motto: "Order. Efficiency. Evolution."*

The largest and most operationally influential guild in current record. Aethelgard functions as a multi-national institutional structure — salaried employment, clear internal rank progression, standardized operational protocols, and benefits packages that make it the dominant recruitment destination for graduates of MOD-accredited hunter academies.

---

## Overview

- **Specialization:** General contract operations across all monster tiers. The Consortium does not specialize by elemental affinity but by operational scale; it is the primary guild for large-footprint, multi-team operations on Class IV and Class V Dungeon clearances.
- **MOD Relationship:** Full compliance. Aethelgard maintains the most complete MOD compliance record of any major guild and is the first recipient of MOD's large-contract delegations. Field Operations coordinates directly with Aethelgard on S-rank and above Breach responses when Sentinel capacity is insufficient.
- **Recruitment:** Top graduates from MOD-accredited academies; selective headhunting of successful independents. High Amplitude ratings and disciplined, team-oriented assessment profiles are favored over high-outlier individuals.
- **Public Perception:** Professional, reliable, and faintly institutional. The safe choice for a hunter career. Critics describe them as too structured to take necessary risks; the operational data does not obviously support this characterization, but the reputation persists.

---

## Structure

Aethelgard operates on a corporate model uncommon among guilds: fixed salaries, internal rank titles that parallel but do not map directly to MOD certification ranks, dedicated support staff who are not hunters, and standardized equipment distribution by operational tier. This structure makes it the most legible organization in the hunter world to people who have never been hunters — and the most uncomfortable fit for hunters who chose independence precisely to avoid it.

The Consortium's contract delegation relationship with MOD is its most significant institutional asset. When MOD needs a large-scale operation coordinated without Sentinel involvement, Aethelgard receives the first call. When Aethelgard needs access to restricted Dungeon zones, the compliance record produces it.
