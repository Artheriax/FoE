# 🌌 Fragments of Eternity (FoE) — Master Dashboard

Welcome to the **Fragments of Eternity** worldbuilding repository. This master dashboard connects all primary pillars of the Hyperion universe to help you (and the AI) navigate the lore quickly.

## 🗺️ The World of Hyperion
*Where it all happens.*
- **[Races](world/Races.md)**: The biological and cosmic hierarchy (Mortal → Higher Ones).

## 🌐 The Layers
*The stratified cosmos — deeper = darker, more powerful, more dangerous.*
- **[Layer -1: The Magical Layer](world/Layers/Layer-Minus-1.md)**: First stratum beneath the material universe. Four lords (Fire, Stone, Decay, Sea). Civilizations, trade, tournaments.
- **[Layer -2: The Dark Magic Layer](world/Layers/Layer-Minus-2.md)**: Gothic horror. Black waters, cursed cathedral, three lords (Darkness, Evil/Corruption, Illusion).
- **[Layer -3: The Primal Magic Layer](world/Layers/Layer-Minus-3.md)**: Red desert, primal survival. Three lords (Hunger, War and Rage, Brutality).
- **[Layer -4: The Abyssal Magic Layer](world/Layers/Layer-Minus-4.md)**: Broken reality. Three custodian lords (Distortion, Unstability, Paradox). No inhabitants.

## ⚔️ Systems of Power
*How the cosmos is structured and how mortals break their limits.*
- **[Cosmology](systems/Cosmology.md)**: The Layered Universe — Layer 0, +N, -N; lord system, succession, messengers, champions, tournaments.
- **[Arcane Magic](systems/Arcane.md)**: The schools of magic, from Elemental to Forbidden.
- **[Monsters](systems/Monsters.md)**: Essence harvesting and the mechanics of Forced Growth.

## 🏛️ Institutions & Factions
*Who governs the power.*
- **[Organizations Index](organizations/Organizations.md)**: The primary registry of factions.
- **[Magic Oversight Division (MOD)](organizations/institutions/MOD.md)**: Hyperion's largest corporation; regulates hunters, cross-layer trade, descent licensing, artifact importation, and tournament sanctioning.
