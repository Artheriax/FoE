# History of Hyperion

Hyperion's historical record is incomplete by design. The oldest events predate organized documentation; the most significant are frequently classified. What follows is organized by Era — a framework applied retroactively to periods defined more by their dominant events than by any consistent calendrical structure.

Events are noted with their institutional status where available. Sealed entries appear with classification tags. Contested entries reflect scholarly disagreement rather than institutional ambiguity.

**Era Note:** The Eastern Era (EE) date system appears in some Gaze and Arcane records as supplementary sourcing (e.g., 264 EE = Mid-Era approximate). Modern MOD documentation uses the Era designation system exclusively.

## Era Summaries

**Cosmological Origins** — Artem creates gazes and sigils; residual substrate contaminates groundwater; Higher Ones imprison him in the Wall of Nihility. Events reconstructed from theoretical sources and the Artem case; no documentary record.

**First Era** — Magic transitions from feared aberration to accepted force; gazes and sigils spread through populations along trade routes and river systems; first primitive ability registries appear. No complete documentary record survives; reconstructed from archaeological evidence and registry fragments.

**Pre-Record** — Events before the Era numbering system. Physical evidence only; no living witnesses. Major events: The Second Weave, Cataclysm of Yggdra, Fracturing of the Eastern Rift, Collapse of the Crimson Tower, First Documentation of the Wall of Nihility.

**Second Era** — First institutional records. The Compact Defiant Adjudication (Soul Anchoring origin case). Establishment of what would become the Keepers of Nil.

**Third Era** — MOD registry formalized. Key events: The Veiled Operations, Shadow Magic Case I (Ashenveil Disturbance), Investigation of Malachar, Terthas Clearseeing (Revelation Gaze), The Dual Sigil Incident, The Ironfield Surge.

**Fourth Era** — The Callax Abomination: Soul Forging Case Origin. Eternal Guard forbidden magic protocols set.

**Mid-Era** — The Ashenmoor Campaigns period (informal designation). Events: The Ashenmoor Campaigns, The Colossus (AD-7), Healer Cassian, Glaciarch Frostweaver, Serene of the Ashenveil, Ghastward of Ashenveil, Corven Markward, Shadow Magic Case II (Maltheryx Anchor), The Valley of Ashemark Incident.

**Fifth Era** — The Dreaming of Valcrest (Dream Keystone engagement; subjective-duration protocols established).

**Sixth Era** — The Sixth Era Collapse: Reality Fabrication Catastrophe (Thessalyn/Korven, 47 timelines, 4,000 km² fracture). The Caldenmere Void Reacher (never neutralized; presumed displaced by Collapse).

**Seventh Era** — Siege of Irenmere Pass (Valdren Ashcrast). The Stormcalm Trials (Kinesis→Omnikinesis evolution). Archon Dravenmore: Hexline System Architecture. The Sleeper Incident (sealed/ongoing).

**Modern Era** — Events within living institutional memory. The Stormstep Operations, The Voss Suppression Case, The Scroll Retrieval Attempt (Keepers of Nil / Gaelira / *The Soul of the First*).
